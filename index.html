Available Support

CITL provides pedagogical and technical support for on-campus and online instructors. This support includes:
  • Online resources to assist in the use of technology
  • Demonstrations to provide a broad overview of a technology,
  • Hands-on workshops for direct interaction with the technology
  • Individualized sessions with instructional designers to assist on-campus instructors as they integrate technology into thcourses./

    Using Intelligent Agents in your Course

    The Intelligent Agents tool allows instructors to set-up automatic notifications to be send to themselves and/or students when specified course performance criteria are met. It can be used to both recognize student achievement (ex. a high score on a quiz) and warn of potential problems (ex. lack of online activity or poor performance on a quiz.).     Brightspace (D2L) Course Setup Kit

    CITL has created a Course Setup Kit for Brightspace (D2L). The setup kit includes standard “student focused” information, such as how to navigate and use Brightspace and how to access library support, links to academic regulations and more. In addition, the site has a series of editable pages where faculty can add their own course-specific content. These pages are stylized and designed to meet accessibility standards, and can be easily edited using the editor within Brightspace (D2L). This starter kit provides a great starting point for new faculty wishing to augment their on-campus teaching with effective technology support and an efficient way for experienced instructors to move Word and PDF documents to accessible HTML pages. To support you in implementing the Course Setup Kit for your courses, please contact us for more details.
